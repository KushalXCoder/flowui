## Why Flow UI?

FlowUI is a UI library, to meet the daily developer needs. ShadCN provides components that helps on building components, and there are other libraries providing animated, or beautifullyd designed components.

What, FlowUI aims is to lie between these two places. By, providing components, some built upon shadcn and some from scratch, which are fully customizable and required
by the devs in their everyday flow.
